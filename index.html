Foresight Games

A global community of game designers and players who use games to explore possible futures

What are foresight games?

Foresight games explore possible futures through play. Foresight can range from personal to global, near future to distant future. We're interested in everything from lightly gameful workshops to fully immersive, high fidelity games. We take an inclusive view of both foresight and games and welcome folks who are adjacent or just curious. Come join us!
